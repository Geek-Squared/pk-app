Introduction

Welcome to Positive Konnections

Positive Konnections (PK) is a cool application with a mental health intervention for people infected or affected by HIV. It's private and easily accessible. It does various things which includes:

  1. supporting people who have had a reactive result on an HIV self testing kit.
  2. It helps you talk to your partner or loved one about HIV.
  3. It's a tool that parents and care givers can use to talk to their children about difficult things surrounding HIV infection.
  4. It provides counselling and helps those struggling to accept their HIV diagnosis or those facing other challenges.
  5. It supports people living with HIV to take their medication even when they feel tired or overwhelmed.
